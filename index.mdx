## How it works

1. **Install** — Add the Harpy snippet to your frontend. Harpy begins tracking behavior immediately.
2. **Identify** — Call `harpy.identify()` when a user logs in to tie behavior to a known user.
3. **Enrich** — Optionally send additional attributes from your backend or connected data sources (CDPs, CRMs, analytics).
4. **Query** — When your agent needs context, call the Harpy API to get a behavioral summary for that user.
5. **Act** — Pass the summary into your agent's system prompt. Your agent now knows who it's talking to.
